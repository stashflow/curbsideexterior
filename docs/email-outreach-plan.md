# CURBSIDE Email Outreach Plan

This plan is built to help CURBSIDE stay visible without wearing people out.

## Main Rule

- Send a welcome email immediately when someone joins the list.
- After that, use a seasonal rhythm instead of a fixed weekly blast.
- Every marketing email must include a one-click unsubscribe link.

## Recommended Rhythm

- January: 1 email
- February: 1 email
- March: 1 email
- April: 1 email
- May: 1 email
- June: 1 email
- July: 1 email
- August: 1 email
- September: 1 email
- October: 1 email
- November: 1 email
- December: 1 email

The app also includes a smarter cadence rule:

- Spring and early fall send a little more often
- Winter and late summer send less often
- If someone unsubscribes, stop immediately

## Tone Rules

- Keep it direct
- Keep it useful
- Avoid fake urgency
- Avoid sounding robotic
- Talk like a real local business
